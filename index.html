The Danger that cyberbullying poses

With the advancement of technology and integration of social media into mainstream lifestyle, the impacts of this dramatic change have been both positive and negative. With better accessibilty of communication channels, the problem of trolling arises. Victims face exclusion from online communties and persistent harassment - as a result their self-confidence and self-worth inevitably take a hit. They feel responsible for the bullying they experience and become uncomfortabe seeking help. This ultimately derails their life.

About us

We are a group of young developers, who through this project aim to bring about solidarity among the bullied community.
